Alumni Testimonials

Pavan Nimmagadda


Structures Dept.

I joined when the team was only 3 months old, back in 2016. Honestly, there was nothing apart from our laptop and a few tables. I handled manufacturing and structural simulation of composites and was involved in working with different materials and manufacturing methods. Working in thrustMIT was a fun-filled learning experience with seniors who were very helpful in both academics and team work. I still remember the sleepless nights we spent perfecting Vyom. I was exci- ted to do something outside my academics and meet new people. During my tenure, I got the opportunity to attend the Spaceport America Cup in 2018 and 2019.

Today when I see what the teams is doing, I feel proud of how they have thrived and achieved so much in the past years,

Praveen K Chaubey


Avionics Dept.

Initially, I never thought I will be able to make it through the 3-month taskphase, then I focused on learning every day. There were some hard times when I thought I can't continue but dedication and hard work helped me to sail through.
Within the team my job was inter-departmental, I assisted all the subsystems. I was involved in the design and development of pilot tubes, ignition system, and research of various sensors and electronic equipment.

The team was very supportive and we had a deep bond with each other, I didn't realize how 2 years went by. In our time the whole team had put their heart and soul to launch vyom and now looking at the accomplishment over the years, I am really proud of the team,

Ayushi Jha


Management Dept.

I was really intrigued by thrustMiT as they were bullding something that was quite unique and challenging, especially at undergraduate level. I joined when the project was fairly new and fortunately got to directly interact and work with the seniors who founded this project and the team. Since rocketry was such an untraveled topic back then, there were a lot of challenges and failures that our team had to face before reaching where we are today. Being a part of the team so determined to succeed and constantly working towards the goal helped me learn the most important life values: perseverance & determination.

It's been almost 2 years since I left thrustMIT but to all the juniors who contributed to the project so well thank you for adding another ounce of proud in all of us.
